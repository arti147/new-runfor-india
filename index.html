ABOUT THE EVENT

The Run for Army Marathon, abbreviated as RFAM, is an annual international marathon held in Mumbai, India, on Sunday’s before Republic Day and Independence Day every year from 5.30 am till 9 am. It is a marathon where citizens of India Run to show solidarity and respect for all Army Personnel Protecting Indian Borders, the funds generated thru this event are going to be exclusively used for Girl Child Education / Child Education of martyred Army personal including Army, Navy, Airforce, CRPF or Police personal protecting us citizens 24/7, 365 days a year.

The marathon is an event where people from all walks of life participate including Bollywood celebrities, sports personalities, business tycoons and amateur athletes to show their respect for Army which protects our Indian Borders, so that its citizen can enjoy freedom.
